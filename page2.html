Game Rules & Etiquette [up]
- No Meta Gaming
Act according to how your character would act with what knowledge or assumptions of the situation he/she has currently.

- Give a Heads Up (Play by Post)
When you want to react to a situation in a way that affects its outcome and need time to type out your response send a "!" to let the Players and DM know.

- "Can I do X"
Don't be afraid to ask the DM if a your halfling can attempt to push a boulder off a cliff to squash a group of kobolds, If the action is impossible for your character the DM will let you know, and possibly give you hints for other alternative actions

- Try to be Specific
When describing your actions state exactly what your character does or says, don't just go "I insult/intimidate/seduce him/her/it/them" cause the DM will need something to base off of how the NPC will react
